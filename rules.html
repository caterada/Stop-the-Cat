Game Rules

This will be a game where you will have to stop your cat from climbing the stairs and getting to your food.

You are not home, and looking into the cat cam. You have a gate at the top of the stairs that can close remotely via an app. However, in order to activate it, it needs to check that you are human by making you guess a random five letter word… you are questioning if this gate was worth the $30 you spent, but that’s another story.

Anyways, you must guess the random five letter word. You may guess one letter at a time, and every incorrect guess gets the cat up one flight of stairs. If the cat climbs six flights of stairs, the cat eats your delicious food you were saving for when you got home. If you guess the word correctly, the gate closes and your food is safe.
